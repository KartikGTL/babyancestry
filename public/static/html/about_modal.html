About Baby Ancestry

My goal with Baby Ancestry was to make it easy to get to know your ancestors and find a name for your precious little one! I tried to strip away all the complicated aspects of family history, and just focus on 2 things: The names of your ancestors, and the stories and pictures that help you get to know them.


Remember that this data comes from FamilySearch. It is the best genealogy database in the world, but there can still be errors, especially as you go back farther.


Whether Baby Ancestry guides you to the name you give your child, or just helps you be a little more acquainted with your ancestors, I will feel like my efforts have been a success.


Thanks for using Baby Ancestry, and please leave your feedback if you have comments or suggestions for improvement.

Tyler Patterson
